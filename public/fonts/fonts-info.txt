Note: These font files need to be properly licensed for your project. The files should be placed in:

- public/fonts/Satoshi-Regular.woff2
- public/fonts/Satoshi-Medium.woff2
- public/fonts/Satoshi-Bold.woff2
- public/fonts/IBMPlexMono-Regular.woff2
- public/fonts/IBMPlexMono-Medium.woff2

You can download IBM Plex Mono fonts from Google Fonts, and Satoshi fonts require licensing from the foundry.
